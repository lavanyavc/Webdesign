The most searched countries in November

AUSTRALIA

Australia is the world’s sixth-largest country, covering an area of 7.7 million square kilometers.It is the only continent that is also a country.The capital city of Australia is Canberra.

BALI

Bali is the most popular Indonesian island known for its natural settings, scenic beaches, traditional culture, rich history, creativity, art and crafts, and the genuine warmth of the local people.

DUBAI

Dubai is famous for wealth, being expensive, and being a popular luxury travel destination, but it is also famous for its souks, the Dubai Creek,camel riding, some history and culture.

FRANCE

France is historically recognized as the country of literature, philosophy, luxury and arts… It is also renowned for its technological skills and its great capacity for innovation.Paris is the capital of France.

ITALY

Italy is well-known for its architecture, culture, art, opera, literature, film, and fashion. This is one of the best countries to go on a foreign tour. The country is known for its mesmerizing topography, pristine beaches.

SINGAPORE

Singapore is a city, a capital and a state all at once, making it one of only three City States in the world.Singapore is famous for its melting pot of food hubs, tidiness, and shopping centres.

GERMANY

Germany has been called the “Land of Poets and Thinkers.” Germans are famous in all forms of art, but particularly classical music. Germany's famous composers include Bach, Brahms, Schumann, Wagner and Beethoven.

VIETNAM

Vietnam, known as the land of “Ascending Dragon”, is a one-of-a-kind place. Apart from being a spectacular destination Vietnam is famous for its amazing cuisine and fun activities.
